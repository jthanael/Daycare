Enrollment at Berline Family Daycare

We make the process simple. Here's everything you need to know to get your child started.

💬

Enrollment is handled personally — we believe your first conversation should be with a real person, not a form. Call or message us to check availability and schedule your family's visit. We'll walk you through everything from there.

Getting Started

How Enrollment Works

Simple, personal, and designed around your family.

Reach Out

Call or send us a message to introduce yourself and ask about availability for your child's age group.

Schedule a Tour

Visit in person to see the space, meet the staff, and get a feel for the daily routine. Bring your questions!

Submit Paperwork

We'll provide all required forms during your visit. You'll complete them at your own pace, in person.

Welcome Your Child

Once everything is in order, we'll confirm your start date and prepare a warm welcome for your little one.

What We'll Need From You

This information is required to ensure smooth communication and the safest possible care for your child.

Parent & Guardian Information

  • Parent/Guardian full name
  • Phone number (primary & alternate)
  • Email address
  • Home address
  • Emergency contact name, number & relationship
  • Authorized pickup individuals
  • Child's date of birth and any medical needs
  • Immunization records (required by MA EEC)

What to Bring on Your Child's First Day

  • ✓ Comfortable clothing and one extra set
  • ✓ Favorite blanket or comfort item (optional)
  • ✓ Lunch and snacks (if applicable)
  • ✓ Diapers and wipes (for infants & toddlers)
  • ✓ Any remaining forms given during your tour
  • ✓ Any medications with written instructions

Ready to Get Started?

Availability is limited. Reach out today to check open spots and schedule your family's visit.
