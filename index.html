About Us

Where Children Grow, Explore, and Thrive

Berline Family Daycare is a licensed, home-based daycare serving Fitchburg and the surrounding communities. We believe every child deserves individual attention, a consistent routine, and an environment where curiosity is celebrated.

As a small family daycare, we keep group sizes intentionally small so your child is never just a number. You'll always know exactly how your child's day went — because we take the time to tell you.

Programs Designed for Every Stage

Each program is thoughtfully structured to match your child's developmental needs — from their first days to kindergarten readiness.

👶

Infant Care

6 weeks – 18 months

One-on-one nurturing care during your baby's most important developmental window. We follow your home schedule so transitions are smooth.

  • Individualized feeding & sleep schedules
  • Sensory play and tummy time
  • Daily parent photo updates
  • Low caregiver-to-infant ratio

  • Structured outdoor play

A Typical Day

Structured, Predictable & Full of Learning

Consistent routines build confidence. Here's what your child's day looks like with us.

🌅
7:30 – 9:00 AM

Morning Arrival

Warm greetings, soft music, and free exploration with puzzles and books while everyone settles in.

🎨
9:00 – 11:00 AM

Learning Activities

Guided lessons, arts & crafts, storytime, music, and age-appropriate games that build early skills.

🍎
11:00 – 11:45 AM

Snack & Circle Time

Nutritious morning snack followed by group conversation, sharing, and social skill building.

🌿
11:45 AM – 12:30 PM

Outdoor Play

Fresh air, supervised physical activity, and exploration in our safe enclosed outdoor space every day.

🍽️
12:30 – 1:15 PM

Lunch

Family-style lunch in a calm, social setting. Children bring lunch from home or per individual arrangement.

😴
1:15 – 3:00 PM

Nap & Quiet Rest

Rest time for infants and toddlers; quiet independent activities for older children who no longer nap.

🧩
3:00 – 4:30 PM

Afternoon Activities

Afternoon snack, sensory play, creative projects, and child-led free play with peers.

👋
4:30 – 5:30 PM

Pickup & Daily Recap

Each parent receives a personal update on their child's day — activities, meals, mood, and milestones.

Note: Schedules are adapted by age group. Infants follow individualized routines. Contact us to discuss your child's specific needs.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We want you to feel completely informed. Here are the questions parents ask most.

We are open Monday through Friday. Please contact us directly for our current operating hours and to discuss whether our schedule works for your family's needs. We're happy to talk through drop-off and pickup flexibility.

The best first step is to reach out via phone or the contact form on this page to schedule a tour. During your visit, you'll meet our staff, see the space, and receive all enrollment paperwork. We review applications and match families based on available spots and your child's age group.

We'll provide a detailed checklist before your child's start date. Generally, parents bring: comfortable clothing and one extra set, any required medications with written instructions, lunch and snacks (if applicable), a favorite comfort item, and any outstanding enrollment forms.

Please contact us directly to discuss payment and subsidy options. As a licensed MA EEC provider, we can speak to what programs may apply to your situation and help guide you through the process.

Your child's health is our top priority. If a child develops symptoms during the day, we immediately notify the parent or emergency contact. All staff are current on CPR and First Aid. We follow MA EEC health and illness policies to protect all children in our care.

Yes! We have an open-door policy and welcome parent visits. We believe transparency builds trust. Just give us a heads up so we can ensure minimal disruption to the children's routine, and you're always welcome to see what a typical day looks like.
